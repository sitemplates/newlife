Welcome To Seventh-Day Adventist Church Newlife Nairobi

Newlife Seventh-day Adventist Church Nairobi is a diverse Christian community comprised of individuals that seek to love God, each other and the greater community.

Our church is a part of a worldwide community of Seventh-day Adventists. We share a love for Jesus and a commitment to demonstrating and spreading the good news of His transforming love to our neighbourhoods, the city of Nairobi, and the world.

All are welcome.

Christian greetings and welcome to Newlife church

23rd April 2022

Special Greetings and Welcome to Newlife Church!

This Sabbath, we continue to celebrate our Chaplaincy Services with special focus on Hospital Ministry.

Hospitality Chaplaincy is a compassionate ministry inspired by Matthew 25:31-46.

“Not only are they to care for the physical needs of the patients but are to bring people into contact with the great physician. Lift up their thoughts to Jesus. Plant in their mind uplifting and ennobling thought … Speak of the hope of the gospel. Hold up before them the promises of God’s word. Remind them of their responsibility in healthful living”. – Ellen G White, Ministry of Healing p. 120-122.

As we reflect on this noble ministry, its vision and import, may we be drawn to The Great Physician who grants us the Ultimate healing.

Pr. Jacob Akali

1st April 2022

Friends & Members of Newlife, Happy Sabbath!

We are glad that you have joined us in the Lord’s House of Worship today. Feel welcome and may God bless you.

A special welcome to the 23 members who joined the church through baptism at the conclusion of the Jesus Saves Evangelistic campaign.

We thank God for those who followed the program online and in-person – may the Lord keep you till the day of His coming.

Today we will share in Holy Communion (Lord’s Supper), Prayer & Fasting and the culmination of the Youth Week of Prayer.

The Lord’s Supper is a participation in the emblems of the sinless body and blood of Jesus as an expression of faith in Him, our Lord and Saviour. In this experience of communion, the Holy Spirit is present to meet and strengthen God’s people.

As we partake, we joyfully proclaim the Lord’s death until He comes again.

Sabbath Blessings as you worship with us.

Pr. Jacob Akali

5th March 2022

Happy Sabbath saints and welcome to Women’s Ministry Sabbath. May we all be blessed.

Women should not feel that they are excused because of their domestic cares. They should become intelligent as to how they can work most successfully and methodically in bringing souls to Christ.

Women of firm principle and decided character are needed, women who believe that we are indeed living in the last days and that we have the last solemn message of warning to be given to the world.

They should feel that they are engaged in the important work of spreading the rays of light which Heaven has shed upon them.

When the love of God and His truth is an abiding principle, they will let nothing deter them from duty, or discourage them in their work. They will fear God, and will not be diverted from their labours in His cause by the temptation of lucrative situations and attractive prospects.

They will preserve their integrity at any cost to themselves.

These are the ones who will correctly represent the religion of Christ, whose words will be fitly spoken, like apples of gold in pictures of silver. Such persons, in many ways, can do precious work for God. He calls upon them to go out into the harvest field, and help gather in the sheaves. – DG 17.2

Pr. Jacob Akali

12th March 2022

Dear friends of God, happy Sabbath and welcome so much to our Evangelistic Sabbath worship. Thank you dearly for choosing to be part of this beautiful spiritual family.

We love, cherish and appreciate your presence and support and it is our prayers that you will receive an abundance of heavenly blessings.

Evangelism is the very heart of Christianity. It is a theme of primary importance to those called to herald God’s last warning to a doomed world. We are in time’s closing hours, and the Advent Message – proclaimed to make ready a people prepared for our Lord’s return – must swell to a loud cry reaching the uttermost parts of the earth.

“Let us now take up the work appointed us, and proclaim the message that is to arouse men and women to a sense of their danger. If every Seventh-day Adventist had done the work laid upon him, the number of believers would now be much larger than it is”. EV. 17:3.

“You are ambassadors for Christ, to proclaim His message of salvation.” Ev 22 {2 Cor 5:20}.

Learn your call, work and pray to meet the assignment.

Happy Sabbath.

Pr. Jacob Akali

19th March 2022

Dear friends of Jehovah, happy Sabbath and welcome dearly to our second Evangelistic Sabbath worship. Thank you for choosing to be part of this beautiful spiritual family.

We are much grateful for your prayers, presence, contributions, and sacrifices towards the marvellous achievements of our first week of Evangelistic meeting and we are looking forward to the same as we enter the final week of our meetings, believing that many souls will be led to Christ the mighty to save.

Remember you have a personal responsibility! “God could have reached His objects in saving sinners without our aid; but for us to develop a character like Christ’s, we must share in His work.

In order to enter into His joy, the joy of seeing souls redeemed by His sacrifice, we must participate in His labours for their redemption.” DA. 142.

Finally, remember you’re born a Disciple into the Kingdom / Church of God!

“Every True Disciple is born into the kingdom of God as a missionary. He who drinks of the living water becomes a fountain of life. The receiver becomes a giver.” DA. 195.

May the heavenly showers of blessings be your portion as we worship together.

Pr. Jacob Akali

24th July 2022

When Jesus gave us the Great Commission in Matthew 28:19, 20 to bring the gospel to all the people of the world, I believe He included children. So while we evangelize to adults, we must not forget that children need the Lord too. Millions of children, especially in the 10/40 Window, have never heard the name of Jesus. We are to bring them to Jesus and prepare them to meet Him when He comes again.

As a church, have we truly listened to Jesus’ teaching about the place of children in the kingdom of God? Children model faith and discipleship by showing us adults how to humbly repent and trust in the God of salvation. When we welcome them, we are accepting and respecting them as Christ Himself did.

Notice that Jesus made it very clear that if we neglect and abuse them or turn them away from faith in Christ, we will face severe judgment from God Himself. Ellen White states in encouraging mission to children that, “It is still true that children are the most susceptible to the teachings of the gospel; their hearts are open to divine influences, and strong to retain the lessons received.” we should not neglect children at all cost.

Have a blessed Sabbath.

Pr. Jacob Akali

31st July 2022

Happy Sabbath Saints!

Today is Adventist Men Ministries Sabbath.

Men have a special responsibility in society as they serve as leaders in their families and as servant-minded role models in their communities.

As men grow in maturity and raise a family, they have an incredibly important role to play as husbands and fathers. In showing loving leadership at home, fathers can give their children a glimpse of the love, patience, compassion and forgiveness of God, our Heavenly Father.

There has never been a more important time for men to live lives guided by moral, biblical values, rooted in a sense of servant leadership. In a world jaded by stories of abuse and neglect at the hands of men, it’s time to model how men of principle should behave. This takes practical, consistent, positive living, not just lip service when people are looking.

Blessed Sabbath.
